cho is played with 7 pawns, 2 tower and 1 queen
players start in this position, on opposite sides:
	  P
	P T P
	P Q P
	P T P
	
verticality is defined as going away/in the direction of the center of the board
horizontality is defined as the circular movement around the center of the board
	
rules:
	the queen and the pawns can move one tile vertically or horizontally
	pawns that reach the center of the board turn into towers
	towers that reach the center of the board turn into pawns
	towers can move an infinite amount of tiles, vertically or horizontally
	only pawns can cross vertically from one colored column to another
	if one of the queens die, that players lose
	if a queen reaches the center of the board, that player wins
